About ApolloX

The UAE processes over $160B in annual foreign inflows, much of it through informal FX dealers and outdated banking rails. ApolloX bridges licensed fiat infrastructure with blockchain-inspired settlement rails to modernize inflows while meeting the highest regulatory standards.
